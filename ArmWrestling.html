ARM WRESTLING
Event Overview

The Armwrestling Competition is an intense test of strength, technique, and endurance. Participants will face off in a knockout format, with each match being a best-of-three rounds. Competitors must follow strict form and fair play, as officiated by two referees. Do you have the power to dominate the table and claim victory?

Rules and Regulations
