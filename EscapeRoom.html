Rules and Regulations

  • ♠️ Inspired by Alice in Borderland—outthink, outplay, escape! ⏳🔥

  • 📌 Team Size: 4 players per team. Cooperation is key to survival!

  • ⏳ Time Limit: You have 20 minutes to escape. Use your time wisely!

  • 🚫 No External Help: Phones, smart devices, or outside assistance are not allowed. The only way out is through your wits!

  • 🔍 Respect the Game: Do not force open locks, damage props, or tamper with anything not part of the game. Everything you need is within reach.

  • 🔹 4 Rounds of Challenges involving different skills:
    1. 💻 Computer – Solve logic-based tasks, puzzles, or basic tech-related problems (no coding required!).
    2. 🔢 Pattern Solving – Identify hidden sequences and crack the codes.
    3. ⚡ Electronics Puzzles – Simple circuit challenges like connecting wires in the right way.
    4. 🧠 Logical Reasoning & Strategy – Think critically, make decisions, and navigate unexpected twists.
