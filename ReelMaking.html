Event Overview

The Techfest 2.0 Reel Editing Competition is a creative challenge where participants showcase their video editing skills by creating a 30 to 60-second promotional reel . Participants must focus on cinematography, editing, and creativity, ensuring their reel is visually appealing and engaging. The competition will take place on Instagram, where entries will be judged based on technical quality, creativity, and engagement.
