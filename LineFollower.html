LINE FOLLOWER

Event Overview

The Line Follower Competition challenges teams to design and program an autonomous robot that follows a designated path. The robot must navigate the track efficiently without deviation while maintaining accuracy and speed. The competition tests the bot's precision in path tracking and the team's ability to optimize its performance.

Rules and Regulations

  • The overall specifications of the bot must not exceed the following dimensions:
    • Length: 50cm
    • Width: 22cm
    • Height: 20cm
    • Weight: 5kg
    • Maximum Voltage: 12V
  • Main track line width: 25mm

  • All the team members must carry their college ID proof on the day of the event.

  • LEGO is prohibited. Ready-made bots are strictly restricted.

  • A team can comprise a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • Team members cannot be part of more than one team.

  • Each team will get 2 trials. The best of these 2 trials will be judged.
  • Each team is allowed a test run of maximum 5 minutes (before the 1st trial) to test their bot on the main track and fix any issues. The 1st trial will start immediately afterward.
  • Test mats will be provided, but the line width may vary from the main mat.
  • The 2nd trial can be taken right after the 1st trial or later during the competition.

  • If the bot de-rails, it must be placed exactly where it de-railed. Placing it elsewhere may lead to disqualification.

  • Maximum 8 hand-touches are allowed. Exceeding this limit results in disqualification.
    • Each hand-touch incurs a 5-second penalty.

  • No pushing the bot is allowed. Only 90-degree readjusting touches are permitted.

Judging Criteria:

  • The winner is determined based on the fastest completion time while strictly following the designated track.
  • If no bot completes the track, the one that reaches the most checkpoints in the shortest time qualifies for the final round.
  • Any shortcut or deviation from the track will result in the bot being placed back at the nearest checkpoint.
