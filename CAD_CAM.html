Event Overview

The CAD Competition is a test of precision, drafting skills, and design expertise. Participants will use SolidWorks 2013 to create a detailed technical drawing within a 1.5-hour time limit . The competition evaluates the ability to follow standard drafting practices, accuracy in representation, and overall visual presentation.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Each student is allowed only one entry in the competition.

  2. Participants must use standard CAD equipment for drafting.

  3. The competition will be conducted using SolidWorks 2013 software.

  4. Entries will be accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.

  5. Drawing Requirements:
    • A complete drawing must be submitted at the end of the competition.

  6. Time Allotment:
    • Participants will have a maximum of 1.5 hours to complete their drawings.

  7. All participants are expected to adhere to these rules to ensure a fair and competitive environment.
