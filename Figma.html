Event Overview

The Figma Design Competition is a time-bound challenge where participants must create an original UI/UX design using Figma . Designers will be assigned a topic at the start of the event and must conceptualize, design, and present their project within the given timeframe. The event tests creativity, design aesthetics, and usability principles.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Team Composition & Duration:
    • Team Size: 1-2 members.
    • Total Design Time: 3 hours.

  2. Figma Account Requirement:
    • Participants must create a Figma account on the day of the event.
    • All design work must be done in Figma‚Äîother design tools are not allowed.

  3. Originality & Fair Play:
    • No pre-made templates or UI kits are allowed.
    • AI-generated UI designs are strictly prohibited‚Äîall work must be original.
    • Legally available images (e.g., Unsplash, Pexels) can be used if they fit the theme.

  4. Topic Selection:
    • Topics will be randomly assigned via chit-picking at the start of the competition.
    • Participants must design strictly according to the assigned topic.

  5. Design Guidelines:
    • Participants can design for any digital interface (mobile app, web app, desktop software, etc.).
    • Functional prototyping is encouraged but not mandatory.
    • The final submission must be made within the time limit‚Äîno edits allowed after submission.

  6. Presentation:
    • After designing, each team gets 5 minutes to present their project.
    • The presentation should cover:
      • Concept & Inspiration.
      • Design Process.
      • Usability & Functionality.
