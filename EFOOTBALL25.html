E FOOTBALL 25
Event Overview

The E FOOTBALL Tournament is a competitive gaming event where participants test their football skills in an intense virtual showdown. Players will go head-to-head in knockout matches, battling for victory in timed rounds. Fair play, strategy, and quick decision-making will determine the ultimate champion.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Match Time: 8 minutes

  2. Extra Time & Penalties: Allowed in matches

  3. Team Strength: Unlimited

  4. Player Cards: Any player cards can be used

  5. Number of Substitutes: 6

  6. Game Conditions:
    • Must be set to [Random], ensuring the same conditions for both home and away teams.

  7. Match Settings:
    • If incorrect settings are found, the game must be restarted immediately before continuing.

    • Complaints after the match will not be considered.

  8. Backpass Rule:
    • No time-wasting after gaining a lead.

    • If reported by the opponent, strict action will be taken by the organizers.

  9. Respect & Sportsmanship:
    • Players must respect their opponents at all times.

    • Foul language or unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated.

  10. Final Decision: Any disputes or unforeseen issues will be resolved by the organizers, and their decision will be final.

    • Match Outcome:
      • The player who wins the match advances to the next round.
      • In case of a draw, extra time and penalties will be used to determine the winner.

      • No backpass gameplay (wasting time after taking the lead is strictly prohibited).
      • Proper match settings must be used. Any errors in match settings must be identified before playing, or the game must be restarted.

    • Organizers' Final Decision:
      • Any issues outside the stated rules will be resolved by the organizers.
