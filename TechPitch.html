TECH PITCH
Event Overview

Do you have a groundbreaking business or innovation idea ? Pitch Tech gives you the stage to present, impress, and inspire! Showcase your creativity, feasibility, and long-term impact in a structured pitch. A prototype demo is highly encouraged to strengthen your concept!

Rules and Regulations

  1. ⏳ Time Limit: 8 minutes per team for the pitch.

  2. 📊 Mandatory PowerPoint Presentation must include:
    • Idea Title & Description
    • Revenue Model – How will it make money?
    • Challenges & Solutions – Address potential hurdles.
    • Future Scope – Vision for growth & scalability.

  3. 💡Prototype Presentation Encouraged! Boost credibility with a working model.

  4. Engaging, clear, and impactful delivery is key!

  5. Think big. Pitch smart. Innovate for the future! 🚀

    6. Judging Criteria:

    1. Clarity & Structure – Well-organized and easy to understand.
    2. Innovation – Unique idea with a competitive edge.
    3. Revenue Model – Financial sustainability & growth potential.
    4. Challenges & Solutions – Awareness of obstacles and strategies.
    5. Future Scope – Scalability and long-term vision.
    6. Overall Presentation – Engaging delivery with supporting visuals.
