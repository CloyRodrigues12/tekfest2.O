FUN WITH CODING

Event Overview

Think you have what it takes to outthink, outcode, and outlast ? This coding competition is designed to test your logical reasoning, problem-solving skills, and coding expertise in C and C++ . With a mix of MCQs, surprise challenges, and hands-on coding tasks, only the sharpest minds will claim victory!

Rules and Regulations

  1. Team Composition:
    • Each team must consist of 2 members from the same institute.
    • A maximum of 20 teams will be allowed to participate.

  2. Competition Guidelines:
    • Programming languages allowed: C, C++ only.
    • Participants must report 15 minutes before the event starts.
    • Late arrivals will not be given extra time.
    • External resources, online references, or external help are strictly prohibited.
    • Any form of malpractice (plagiarism, unauthorized communication, code-sharing) will result in immediate disqualification.
    • Judges' decisions are final in all aspects of evaluation, including problem-solving approach and efficiency.
    • Organizers reserve the right to amend rules if necessary, with prior communication.

  3. Round Details:
    • Round 1: MCQ Round
      • Topics: General Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Programming (C & C++).
      • Time-limited quiz to test fundamental skills.
    • Round 2: Surprise Round!
      • Details will be revealed on the event day!
    • Round 3: Coding Challenge (HackerRank)
      • Hands-on coding challenge on HackerRank.
      • Solve algorithmic problems within a time limit.

Judging Criteria:

  • Logical Thinking & Problem-Solving Approach
  • Code Efficiency & Optimization
  • Accuracy & Correctness of Solutions
  • Time Taken to Solve Challenges
